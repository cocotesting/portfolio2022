Expense Tracker

Introduction

A web app helps to track your financial activity by recording your incomes and tracking where your money is going.
The simple design contains all essential items which is easy to use and it includes 3 components:

  1. Option: Automatically categorizes the records once you inserted the income or expense categories.
  2. Income & Expense: Easily records the transaction to track where your money comes and goes.
  3. Report: Provides detail monthly reports of your financial activity including income and expense by time and category.
